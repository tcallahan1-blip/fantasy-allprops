# Fantasy AllProps — League Rules
Season 1: August 2026 – July 2027

---

## Overview

Fantasy AllProps is a year-long prediction league spanning 12 categories of real-world events — sports, politics, awards, reality TV, culture, and more. Players compete by making picks across three pick windows, accumulating points as outcomes resolve throughout the season.

**Season 1 window:** August 2026 → July 2027
**Total catalogued events:** 195 across 12 categories

---

## Pick Windows

### Pre-Season Picks (P)
Submitted once before the season opens (late July 2026). These cover long-horizon outcomes — championship winners, award show results, political elections — where a full season of signal is the edge.

- Window opens: ~1 July 2026
- Window closes: Season kick-off (1 August 2026)
- Cannot be changed once locked
- Higher base point value to reflect commitment

### Monthly Picks (M)
Submitted at the start of each calendar month. These are month-specific events — major tournaments, one-off competitions — where recent form and context matter.

- Window opens: 1st of each month at 00:01 UTC
- Window closes: Midnight UTC on the 7th of each month
- Cannot be changed once the monthly window closes

### Pop Props (X)
Short-window, event-specific picks that open and close around a specific moment. These are the league's "live" layer — viral events, weekend showdowns, finale nights.

- Window duration: set per event (typically 24–72 hours before the event)
- Announced in the app and via push notification when window opens
- High-variance, high-engagement picks

## Scoring

*[Mechanics in draft — to be finalized before Season 1 lock]*

**Base point values by window:**
- Pre-season pick correct: **15 pts**
- Monthly pick correct: **10 pts**
- Pop prop correct: **5 pts**

**Bonus windows:** Certain months with dense event clusters trigger a bonus multiplier. Correct picks in a bonus window earn 1.5× the standard value.

**Verification types:**
- **Automated (A):** Result pulled from an official API or data source. Points credited within 24 hours of event completion.
- **Manual (M):** Commissioner verifies the result. Points credited within 48 hours.

**Proper props:** Events marked as research-hard/niche carry the same point values but count toward a "Proper Props" completion badge at season end.

## Verification & Appeals

- Results are sourced from official governing bodies, APIs, or broadcast footage
- Players may submit an appeal within 48 hours of a result being posted
- Commissioner decision is final
- Manual picks (38% of catalogue) are confirmed by the commissioner before points are awarded
